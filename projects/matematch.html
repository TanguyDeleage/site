Matematch

Matematch is a Progressive Web App helping international students in Nantes, who want to discover their surroundings and the local culture, to connect with locals to share unique experiences. Matematch allows them to meet the perfect local guide.

Challenge

In 10 days we must build our very first Progressive Web App (a mobile application accessible from the web). To accomplish this task we will work in a team of 3.

Problem

Every year many young internationals arrive in Nantes for their studies. Due to a lack of informations and opportunities a lot of them miss the chance to meet French people, practice the language or discover the local surroundings. They need a simple way to connect with French people.

Discovery

I wanted to meet potential future users, and understand their needs. Thanks to a previous work in a student association for Erasmus students, I have been able to meet exchange students during interviews. I learned how they are experiencing their stay in France. Even if they want to speak French, when the person is not an English speaker it can become hard to communicate. It's really hard for them to join a group of French people. They would sometimes need a French buddy to help them in different tasks. On the other hand, I did some research about Nantes, and its international culture : Nantes host more than 50 000 students each year. I discovered some events on Facebook named "Café des langues" where you meet internationals to talk in another languages. There are some associations in Nantes which allows French students to help internationals. My conclusion is that Nantes is a place where there are a lot of students that would love to meet and help internationals to discover the French culture, but they struggle to meet and coordinate. In order to solve this problem we choose to create Matematch.

Design

We started by creating a user journey, which leads us to the creation of our first user flow. With this user flow, we have been able to build our very first wireframe, that later evolved to a mockup.

Development

With the design and the user flow done, we were able to build our database schema, that allowed us to build our database, a necessary step to build the app. Few days later our simple user flow became an app, suitable to use on phone.

Launch

After 10 days, it was the moment to pitch our app. In front of 80 people, we realised our product pitch.

Discover the product pitch in video

Impact

Today Matematch is a working Progressive Web App. We are still working on it to add some features before releasing it. However, this challenge was a great experience that taught us the different steps during the creation of a product, and how to deal wih it as a team and in a short time. On a personal aspect, it confirmed my wish to continue my professional path in Product Design.
