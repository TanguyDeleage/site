Les 400 Coop

Les 400 Coop, is a self-managed, cooperative and fair grocery store in Paris.

Challenge

As a cooperator, I want to provide a new design for the public website.

Problem

The coop can work only if there are enough people helping to run it, but also enough people doing their groceries there. The financial team noticed that the turnover is decreasing and the number of active member as well. To save the coop, we started a recruitment campaign. When I wanted to join the coop, I noticed there was a lot of information on the website and it was kind of hard to find the right info. Since the communication is really important to attract new people, I thought I would redesign the website.

Discovery

In the discovery process, before interviewing any users, I chose to do an analysis of every pains I encounter on the website when navigating on it. My first analysis is that the home page is full of informations of different importances (timetables, documentations, link to register, how it works, historical facts). Some links and informations are displayed several times and in various formats. In my opinion it seems essential to work on a simple flow that will help someone to register and get the informations he needs.

Design

I started to work on a potential new homepage. For that I listed every informations on the home page, I gave a priority to each of them. All the high priority informations, and related to acquire a new member needed to be display on the main page. I choose to regroup all of this info in a one page format, with 4 sections.
