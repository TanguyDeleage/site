Nice to meet you

I’m a freelance Product Designer and I'm passionate by people, helping them face their problems thanks to digital products. I have a lot of passions I try to incorporate to my work. I love low technologies and minimalistic lifestyle. Art is also a real inspiration for me, I mainly play music, analog (piano, bass, ukulele...) and digital (Ableton on my computer); and I also make my way into the analog photography. My inspiration also comes from the people I meet, that's why I travel with the closest possible proximity of the locals. For my ideas I also need to have a clear mind, and sports helps me in that way. I do bouldering, yoga and ride different kind of boards (skate, surf, snowboard).
