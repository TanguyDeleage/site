Nice to meet you

I'm a French freelance Product Designer and I'm passionate by entrepreneurship. During my studies I always wanted to launch businesses myself, but really soon I realised that without technical skills, my ideas were just ideas. I joined Le Wagon a coding bootcamp to transform these ideas into products. There I fell in love with Product Design, and changed my career path.

Today I help entrepreneurs build their products (from an idea to a prototype they can present). Thanks to the business aspect of my path, I learnt how to create a product strategy fitting users need. Then through my Product Design position and Le Wagon bootcamp, I learnt how to build a product from the prototype to the development.

Hope to meet you soon.
