My Methodology

01

Strategy

We analyze your initial situation to determine your objectives, painpoints and requirements.

02

Wireframe

We create a wireframe, the simplest version of your project.

03

Discovery

Thanks to the wireframe, we interview your early adopters, and verify if it fits the needs, otherwise we adjust it.

04

User experience

Thanks to the previous phase we now understand how to build your product, we will then build the user journey and the user flow

05

Prototype

We adjust our previous work to fit perfectly to the users and I create an interactive prototype, you'll be able to use to present to investors, test with user, or give to a developper (to save time and money).

06

Development

I build your website or product with code, if a different choice is made, I can help to integrate my prototype with the developper you choose.
