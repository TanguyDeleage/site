My Methodology

01

Discovery

We analyze your initial situation to determine your objectives, painpoints and requirements.

02

Strategy

We define your value proposition. Select your personas, and set a business model.

03

Wireframe

We conceive your user flow and user journey. I create your wireframe based on our discovery phase, to be challenge by users.

04

User tests

We test our wireframe through a workshop with future users, to verify every requirement is check.

05

Prototype

After the test workshop, we adjust our work to fit perfectly to the users and I create an interactive prototype.

06

Leverage

I help you prepare your product pitch thanks to your prototype. I can also work with developpers to help them integrate the prototype into your final product.
